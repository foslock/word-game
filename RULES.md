# Theme Words — Rules

## Objective

Each day a new **Theme Word** is revealed. Hidden beneath it are three mystery words — a 4-letter word, a 5-letter word, and a 6-letter word — each connected to the theme in a unique way.

Your goal is to guess all three words.

---

## How to Play

1. **Look at the Theme Word** displayed at the top of the puzzle. It is your only clue to start.

2. **Fill in the squares.** Each row belongs to one mystery word. The row length tells you how many letters the word has. Type letters into the squares — on desktop use your keyboard, on mobile tap the grid to bring up the keyboard.

3. **Press Submit** (or hit **Enter / Return**) once every unlocked square has a letter.

4. **Read the colours:**

   | Colour | Meaning |
   |--------|---------|
   | 🟩 Green | That letter is in the correct position. It locks in and cannot be changed. |
   | 🟨 Yellow | That letter appears somewhere in one of the three words, but not in this position. |
   | ⬛ Grey | That letter does not appear in any of the three words. |

5. **Keep guessing.** Adjust the letters that aren't locked and submit again. Repeat until all three words are solved.

6. **When a word is fully correct** the row animates green and a sentence is revealed explaining how that word connects to the Theme Word.

7. The puzzle is complete when all three words are solved.

---

## Tips

- The **Theme Word** is a strong hint — think broadly about what concepts it might connect to.
- A **yellow letter** could belong to *any* of the three words, not just the row you typed it in. Use this to your advantage.
- **Locked green letters** cannot be edited — only the remaining squares are in play.
- There is no guess limit. Take as many attempts as you need.
- Your **attempt count** is tracked as your score — fewer is better.

---

## Daily Rhythm

- One new puzzle is available each day.
- The puzzle **resets at midnight**, local time.
- Your progress (including partially filled squares) is saved automatically. If you close the browser and come back later the same day, you'll pick up exactly where you left off.
- Your all-time stats — games played, win rate, streaks, and best score — are also stored locally.

---

## Scoring

Your score for each puzzle is the total number of times you pressed Submit before solving all three words. A perfect game has a low score; there is no penalty for taking more attempts.

*Good luck!*
